Requested OAuth Scopes

BurnCut requests two scopes for one purpose — one-click publishing to the user's own YouTube channel.
U
youtube.upload
Publish AI-generated short-form videos to the authenticated user's own YouTube channel — only on explicit user action, only as private.
R
youtube.readonly
Verify the connected channel and reflect connection status in BurnCut's UI. No content listing, copying, or redistribution.
No background uploads. Every publish requires an explicit click. Tokens are encrypted at rest and revocable at any time.
